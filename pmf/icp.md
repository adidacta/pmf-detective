# Ideal Customer Profile

## Who They Are
Professionals upskilling via YouTube - learning new tools, frameworks, and career skills. They're motivated to grow but time-conscious about their learning investment.

## What They Experience
- **Pain:** YouTube learning feels like not the best use of their time. They try 2x playback to speed up but it feels less effective.
- **Current solution:** Using separate notes apps (Notion, Google Docs, Obsidian) with manually typed timestamps - lots of context switching and friction.

## What They Want
Learn faster. Efficiency is the priority - get more value from the time they invest in YouTube learning.
