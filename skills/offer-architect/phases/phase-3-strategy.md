# Phase 3: Strategy Development

## Goal
Generate a tailored strategy based on the chosen approach.

## Process

1. Read the appropriate strategy file:
   - Cold outreach → `strategies/cold-outreach.md`
   - Organic content → `strategies/organic-content.md`
   - Paid ads → `strategies/paid-ads.md`

2. Generate strategy using ICP, VP, narrative, and mini-product

3. Present with copy/templates

## Output Format

### For Cold Outreach

```
**Cold Outreach Strategy**

**Target:** [ICP description]
**Goal:** [What success looks like]
**Platform:** LinkedIn / Email / Both

**LinkedIn DM Template:**
---
[Personalized opener using their content/post]

[Connection to their pain point]

[Brief value proposition]

[Soft CTA - question or offer]
---

**Email Template:**
---
Subject: [Subject line]

[Email body with same structure]
---

**Outreach Plan:**
- Daily target: X messages
- Response goal: X%
- Follow-up timing: [When to follow up]

**Personalization Tips:**
- [Tip 1]
- [Tip 2]
```

### For Organic Content

```
**Organic Content Strategy**

**Platform:** [Best fit for ICP]
**Posting Frequency:** [X times per week]
**Content Pillars:**
1. [Pillar 1 - problem-focused]
2. [Pillar 2 - solution-focused]
3. [Pillar 3 - social proof/results]

**Post Templates:**

**Hook Post:**
---
[Hook that grabs attention]
[Problem agitation]
[Your unique take]
[CTA to mini-product]
---

**Story Post:**
---
[Personal/customer story]
[Lesson learned]
[How your solution helps]
[CTA]
---

**Value Post:**
---
[Actionable tip related to their pain]
[Why it works]
[CTA to learn more]
---
```
