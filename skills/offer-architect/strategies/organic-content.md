# Organic Content Strategy Guide

## When to Use
- Already familiar with your ICP
- Have an existing network/audience
- Want to build long-term authority
- Limited budget but have time
- Want content to compound over time

## Platforms

### LinkedIn
- Best for B2B professionals
- Algorithm favors engagement
- Text posts perform well
- Document/carousel posts for deeper content

### Twitter/X
- Fast-paced, frequent posting
- Threads for longer content
- Great for tech/startup audiences
- Building in public works well

### YouTube
- Long-form authority content
- Searchable (SEO benefits)
- Requires more production
- Strong for tutorials/education

### Newsletter (Substack/Beehiiv)
- Direct relationship with audience
- Own your subscriber list
- Regular touchpoints
- Can monetize later

## Content Pillars

Structure your content around 3-4 pillars:

1. **Problem Content** - Validate and agitate the pain
2. **Solution Content** - Share your unique approach
3. **Authority Content** - Case studies, results, expertise
4. **Personal Content** - Behind the scenes, journey, lessons

## Post Frameworks

### The Hook Post
```
[Provocative statement or question]

Here's why:
• Point 1
• Point 2
• Point 3

[CTA - follow for more / link to mini-product]
```

### The Story Post
```
[Year] I was [situation].

I tried [common solution] but [it didn't work because].

Then I discovered [your insight].

Now I [result].

Here's what I learned:
[Lesson]

[CTA]
```

### The Value Post
```
[X] ways to [achieve outcome]:

1. [Tip + brief explanation]
2. [Tip + brief explanation]
3. [Tip + brief explanation]

Which one will you try first?

[CTA to mini-product for deeper help]
```

### The Myth-Buster Post
```
"[Common belief]"

Wrong.

Here's what actually works:

[Your contrarian take with evidence]

[CTA]
```

## Content Calendar

**Week 1-2 Schedule:**
- Mon: Problem content
- Wed: Value/tip content
- Fri: Story/personal content

**Engagement time:** 30 min before and after posting

## Metrics to Track

- Impressions/reach
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Profile visits
- Follower growth
- Link clicks
- Mini-product signups
