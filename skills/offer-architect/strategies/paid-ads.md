# Paid Ads Strategy Guide

## When to Use
- Need to reach broader audience quickly
- Have budget ($100-500+ to start)
- Want to test messaging at scale
- ICP is well-defined for targeting
- Ready to iterate based on data

## Platforms

### Facebook/Instagram
- Broadest reach
- Best for B2C and some B2B
- Powerful targeting options
- Visual-focused

### LinkedIn
- Best for B2B
- Higher CPM but quality leads
- Job title/company targeting
- Expensive but precise

### Google Ads
- Intent-based (people searching)
- Works when problem is known
- Can be expensive in competitive niches
- Search + Display options

### TikTok
- Younger demographics
- Lower CPM currently
- Video required
- Authentic content performs best

## Campaign Structure

### Awareness Campaign
- Goal: Get in front of ICP
- Budget: 40% of total
- Content: Value-first, problem-aware

### Consideration Campaign
- Goal: Engage interested people
- Budget: 40% of total
- Content: Solution-focused, testimonials

### Conversion Campaign
- Goal: Drive action
- Budget: 20% of total
- Content: Clear CTA, urgency

## Ad Copy Frameworks

### Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS)
```
Headline: [Problem statement]

Body:
[Agitate the pain]
[Present the solution]
[CTA]
```

### Before-After-Bridge (BAB)
```
Headline: [Before state]

Body:
Imagine [After state].
[Bridge - how to get there]
[CTA]
```

### Social Proof
```
Headline: "[Quote from customer]"

Body:
[Brief context]
[Result they achieved]
[CTA to get same result]
```

## Targeting Guidelines

### Facebook/Instagram
- Interests related to ICP
- Behaviors (purchase behavior, device)
- Lookalike audiences (once you have data)
- Exclude existing customers

### LinkedIn
- Job titles
- Company size
- Industry
- Skills
- Groups

## Creative Guidelines

### Images
- Faces perform well
- Clear, uncluttered
- Brand colors if established
- Mobile-first (most traffic)

### Video
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Captions (most watch muted)
- 15-30 seconds for ads
- Authentic > polished

### Carousel
- Story arc across cards
- Each card stands alone
- CTA on last card
- 3-5 cards optimal

## Budget Allocation

**Testing Phase ($300-500):**
- 3-5 ad variations
- $10-20 per ad
- Run 3-5 days minimum
- Identify winners

**Scaling Phase:**
- Double down on winners
- Test new variations
- Gradually increase budget
- Monitor CPA closely

## Metrics to Track

- CPM (Cost per 1000 impressions)
- CTR (Click-through rate) - aim for 1%+
- CPC (Cost per click)
- Conversion rate
- CPA (Cost per acquisition)
- ROAS (Return on ad spend)
