## Key Page Elements

1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline + Subhead + CTA button
   - Optional: mockup/GIF of product concept

2. **Benefits Section**
   - Timestamped notes while you watch
   - Searchable - find any lesson instantly
   - Portable MD format - you own your notes forever

3. **Email Capture**
   - Simple form: just email
   - Clear value: "Be first to try YouNote"

4. **Social Proof** (add later)
   - Testimonials from beta testers
   - Number of signups ("Join 100+ developers")

## Validation Decision

- **100+ signups** → Validated. Proceed to Sprint 4 (Irresistible Offer)
- **50-99 signups** → Promising. Iterate on messaging or channels
- **<50 signups** → Revisit ICP or value proposition
